Privacy Policy for Secrecy

Last updated: February 10, 2026

Secrecy is an encryption tool that allows you to securely encrypt content on your device and share it via any external app. All encryption happens locally. We only store your public key for account management‚Äîyour private keys never leave your device, and we cannot read your encrypted content.

What is Secrecy?

Secrecy is not a messaging app. It is an encryption tool that provides:

  • Local Encryption: Encrypt content (secrets, notes, messages, files) on your device using public key cryptography
  • Public Key Infrastructure: Each user has a public/private key pair. You encrypt content using someone's public key, and only their private key can decrypt it
  • External Sharing: Share encrypted content via any app you choose (WhatsApp, Telegram, SMS, email, etc.)
  • Secure Storage: Store your encrypted content in the cloud while maintaining zero-knowledge security
  • User Directory: Find other users by username to obtain their public keys for encryption

You remain in full control of your private keys and encrypted content at all times.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, username
  • Profile Information: Full name, Bio
  • Encrypted Content: Your secrets and any other content you use within the app such as notes, bookmarks, etc. (encrypted on your device, we do not have access to your private keys stored in your device)

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device ID for security purposes (to help protect your account from unauthorized access)
  • App Activity: Login timestamps for security monitoring
  • Public Keys: Your public encryption keys (used by others to encrypt content for you). Your private keys remain on your device and are never collected or transmitted

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Authenticate and manage your account
  • Store your public keys to enable encrypted content sharing between users
  • Store your encrypted content securely on our servers
  • Provide encryption and decryption functionality
  • Enable you to find other users by username for secure communication
  • Send important service notifications (app updates, security alerts)

3. How Encryption Works

üîê Local Encryption with Public Key Infrastructure
  • All content is encrypted locally on your device before storage or sharing
  • We use industry-standard encryption: AES-256-GCM and X25519 (ECDH)
  • Your private keys are stored securely on your device and never leave it
  • We only store your public keys for encryption purposes
  • When you encrypt content for someone, it uses their public key
  • Only the recipient with the corresponding private key can decrypt the content
  • We cannot decrypt or read your encrypted content
  • If you lose your private key, we cannot recover your encrypted data

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Service Providers: Supabase (database hosting and authentication) - operates under strict data protection agreements. They only have access to encrypted data and public keys
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or government regulation (note: we can only provide encrypted data and account information, not decrypted content)
  • Public Keys: Your public key may be accessible to other users who search for your username, as this is necessary for them to encrypt content intended for you

What we DON'T do:

  • We do not facilitate or monitor content sharing between users
  • We do not have access to content you share via external apps
  • We cannot see what content you encrypt or who you share it with via external channels

5. How Content Sharing Works

Important: Secrecy is not a messaging app. You encrypt content on your device and share it through external apps of your choice.

When you share encrypted content:

  • Content is encrypted locally on your device using the recipient's public key
  • You choose how to share the encrypted content (WhatsApp, SMS, email, Telegram, or any other app)
  • Secrecy does not transmit or route your encrypted content
  • The external apps you use have their own privacy policies
  • We do not control, monitor, or have access to content shared via external apps
  • Only the intended recipient with the correct private key can decrypt the content
  • Even if intercepted, the encrypted content cannot be read without the recipient's private key

6. Data Storage and Security

What We Store:

  • Account Data: Email address, username, full name, bio
  • Public Keys: Your public encryption keys (accessible to other users)
  • Encrypted Content: Your secrets, notes, and other content (encrypted by you, unreadable by us)
  • Device ID: For security and account protection

Security Measures:

  • Storage Location: Data stored on secure Supabase infrastructure (hosted on AWS)
  • Encryption: All encrypted content is stored encrypted at rest and transmitted encrypted over TLS
  • Private Key Storage: Your private keys are stored exclusively on your device in Android Keystore (hardware-backed security when available)
  • Zero-Knowledge: We cannot decrypt your content as we never have access to your private keys
  • Access Controls: Strict database access rules and row-level security policies
  • Compliance: GDPR and SOC 2 compliant infrastructure

7. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data retained as long as your account is active
  • Account Deletion: All data deleted within 30 days of account deletion request
  • Backups: May persist in backups for up to 90 days

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data associated with your account
  • Delete: Delete individual secrets or your entire account
  • Export: Download your encrypted data
  • Correct: Update your profile information
  • Withdraw Consent: Delete your account at any time

9. Children's Privacy

10. International Data Transfers
