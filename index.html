Privacy Policy for Secrecy

Last updated: February 10, 2026
üõ°Ô∏è Your Privacy Matters

Secrecy uses end-to-end encryption. We cannot read your secrets, passwords, or private data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, username, display name
  • Profile Information: Profile icon selection, color preference
  • Encrypted Content: Your secrets and notes (encrypted on your device)
  • Friend List: Usernames of friends you connect with for key exchange

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device ID for encryption key management
  • App Activity: Login timestamps, app version
  • Encryption Keys: Public keys only (private keys stay on your device)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Authenticate and manage your account
  • Store your encrypted secrets securely
  • Enable sharing of encrypted secrets with friends
  • Provide app functionality and features
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Send important service notifications (app updates, security alerts)

3. End-to-End Encryption

üîê Zero-Knowledge Architecture
  • All secrets are encrypted on your device before being sent to our servers
  • We use industry-standard encryption: AES-256-GCM and X25519 (ECDH)
  • Your encryption keys are stored only on your device
  • We cannot decrypt or read your secrets
  • If you lose your password, we cannot recover your data

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Service Providers: Supabase (database hosting) - operates under strict data protection agreements
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or government regulation
  • User-Initiated Sharing: When you explicitly share encrypted secrets via external apps (WhatsApp, email, etc.)

5. External Sharing

When you share encrypted secrets through external apps:

  • Encrypted data is shared via apps you choose (WhatsApp, SMS, email, etc.)
  • Those apps have their own privacy policies
  • We do not control or monitor external sharing
  • Only the recipient with the decryption key can read the content

6. Data Storage and Security

  • Storage Location: Data stored on secure Supabase infrastructure (AWS)
  • Encryption: All secrets encrypted at rest and in transit
  • Key Storage: Encryption keys stored in device secure storage (Android Keystore)
  • Access Controls: Strict database access rules and authentication
  • Compliance: GDPR and SOC 2 compliant infrastructure

7. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data retained as long as your account is active
  • Account Deletion: All data deleted within 30 days of account deletion request
  • Backups: May persist in backups for up to 90 days

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data associated with your account
  • Delete: Delete individual secrets or your entire account
  • Export: Download your encrypted data
  • Correct: Update your profile information
  • Withdraw Consent: Delete your account at any time

9. Children's Privacy

Secrecy is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored in countries outside your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Sending an in-app notification for significant changes
  • Requiring acceptance for material changes
